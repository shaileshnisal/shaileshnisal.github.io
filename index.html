Meet our Specialists

Dr. Shailesh Nisal

Dr. Nisal is a highly accomplished cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgeon practicing in the city of Nagpur, India. With an experience of more than 20 years, he has been a pioneer in developing the field of plastic surgery in the city of Nagpur. He currently serves as the Director of Sparsh Clinics.


He has specialized, world-class training in aesthetic surgery of the face, breasts, and body. He believes in a highly personalized and compassionate approach to meet the goals of each of his patients. He is an expert in complex reconstructions of the face in both adults and children resulting from congenital, post-traumatic, or cancer deformities.


Dr. Nisal received his MBBS and MS degrees from the prestigious Government medical college, Nagpur and his M.Ch. in Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery from Nair Hospital, Mumbai. He also practiced as a Plastic surgeon at Canberra, Australia and contributed significantly to teaching at Australian National University Medical School.


He is known for his teaching and oratory skills which have garnered him national attention. Subsequently, Dr. Nisal is invited as a speaker at a number of conferences organized by various specialties. He is also popular among plastic surgery trainees and colleagues through his surgical teaching videos on his youtube channel..
